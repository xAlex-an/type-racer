TypeRacer Instructions

How to Take the Test
  1. Select Difficulty: Choose between Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty levels
  2. Start the Test: Click the "Start" button to begin timing
  3. Type the Text: Type the displayed text exactly as shown in the text area
  4. Stop the Test: Click "Stop" button or press Enter to finish
  5. View Results: See your Words Per Minute (WPM), time, and accuracy
  6. Try Again: Click "Retry" for a new sentence at the same difficulty
Real-time Features
  • Blue highlighting: Correctly typed words
  • Red highlighting: Incorrectly typed words
  • Live feedback: See your accuracy as you type
  • Auto-focus: Text area automatically focuses when starting
Important Notes
  • No copy-paste: Pasting is disabled to ensure fair testing
  • Word-by-word scoring: Only completely correct words count toward your WPM
  • Case sensitive: Make sure to match capitalization exactly
  • Punctuation matters: Include all punctuation marks as shown
Difficulty Levels
Easy

Simple sentences with basic vocabulary and common words

Medium

Complex sentences with varied vocabulary and longer texts

Hard

Advanced texts with technical terms and complex sentences

Keyboard Shortcuts
Enter - Stop the current test
Ctrl+V - Disabled (no pasting allowed)
